Help with fictitious organisation

Risk: This RISK ID helps to identify whether an organisation may be fictitious. Purporting to be trading but has been created for deceptive or fraudulent purposes.

  LOW RISK   There is no match for all entities.

  MEDIUM RISK   If there is an exact match for incorporation date and SIC code but a partial match for other entities and the company name has changed within a year.

  HIGH RISK   If there's exact matching details for the incorporation date and organisation address on companies house but the SIC codes and the company name have both changed within a year.

Virtual company registration ID
The organisation is registered at an address where there's a large presence of other companies, this may be a virtual mailbox.
Help with virtual company registration ID

Risk: This RISK ID helps to define the potential thresholds indicating whether a large number of organisations are registered at the same address highlighting the possibility of a virtual mailbox being present.

  LOW RISK   There are less than or equal to 49 registered companies. The organisation is registered at an address where there is minimal chance of a virtual mailbox.

  MEDIUM RISK   There are between 50 and 99 registered companies. The organisation is registered at an address where there may be a virtual mailbox.

  HIGH/MEDIUM RISK   There are between 100 and 249 registered companies. The organisation is registered at an address where there's a large presence of other companies, this may be a virtual mailbox.

  HIGH RISK   There are more than 250 registered companies. The organisation is registered at an address where there's a significant presence of a number of companies, this may be a virtual mailbox.

   MEDIUM RISK  
Impersonation of legitimate company
This organisation is at a low risk of impersonating another organisation.
Help with impersonation of legitimate company

Risk: This risk ID indicates whether an organisation may have a similar organisation name to another organisation but all other company specific details such as the director information or registered trading address or registered address is different. Where the incorporation date of an organisation with a similar name is different. This may indicate the entity is attempting to impersonate another legitimate organisation.

  LOW RISK   If there is a partial match for all entities, the organisation is at a low risk of attempting to clone another legitimate organisation.

  MEDIUM RISK   The organisation has an EXACT or SIMILAR name to another organisation with some partial matching company details; there is a risk the organisation may be attempting to clone another legitimate company.

  HIGH RISK   The organisation has an EXACT or SIMILAR name to another organisation but no other matching company details; there is a risk the organisation may be attempting to clone another legitimate organisation.

   LOW RISK  
Shell company indentification
The organisation displays a significant number of characteristics associated with a Shell company and may be concealing important information and its true purpose and identity.
Help with shell company indentification

Risk: A shell company is an incorporated company with no independent operations, significant assets, ongoing business activities or employees.

  LOW RISK   There are no matches for all entities, The organisation has minimal characteristics that can be associated with a shell company.

  MEDIUM RISK   The company exists but has no assigned business type on companies house and the organisation address has been changed at least twice over the period of 1 year and there is only 1 Directors name on record and there are less than or equal to 1 employee.

  HIGH RISK   The company exists but has no assigned business type on Companies House records and the Organisation Address has been changed at least twice over a period of 1 year and there is only 1 Directors name on record. Also, if the financial turnover for the Company is £0 and there are less than or equal to 1 employee.

   HIGH RISK
