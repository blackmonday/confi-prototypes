Know your applicant

Fictitious organisation
The organisation identified does not match details that could portray it as operating as a fictitious organisation.
Help with fictitious organisation

Risk: This RISK ID helps to identify whether an organisation may be fictitious. Purporting to be trading but has been created for deceptive or fraudulent purposes.

  LOW RISK   The organisation does not have similar details to any organisation that has previously applied for a grant.

  MEDIUM RISK   The organisation has SOME similar details to an organisation that has previously applied for a grant.

  HIGH RISK   The organisation identified has the EXACT match details to an organisation that has previously applied for a grant, this could be a fictitious organisation.

   LOW RISK  
Virtual company registration ID
The organisation is registered at an address where there's a large presence of other companies, this may be a virtual mailbox.
Help with virtual company registration ID

Risk: This RISK ID helps to define the potential thresholds indicating whether a large number of organisations are registered at the same address highlighting the possibility of a virtual mailbox being present.

  LOW RISK   The organisation is registered at an address where there’s minimal chance of a virtual mailbox.

  MEDIUM RISK   The organisation is registered at an address where there may be a virtual mailbox.

  HIGH/MEDIUM RISK   The organisation is registered at an address where there’s a large presence of other companies, this may be a virtual mailbox.

  HIGH RISK   The organisation is registered at an address where there’s a significant presence of a number of companies, this may be a virtual mailbox.

   MEDIUM RISK  
Impersonation of legitimate company
This organisation is at a low risk of impersonating another organisation.
Help with impersonation of legitimate company

Risk: This RISK ID indicates whether an organisation may be attempting to impersonate an existing organisation.

  LOW RISK   The organisation is at low risk of attempting to clone another legitimate organisation.

  MEDIUM RISK   The organisation has an EXACT or SIMILAR name to another organisation with some partial matching company details; there is a risk the organisation may be attempting to clone another legitimate company.

  HIGH RISK   The organisation has an EXACT or SIMILAR name to another organisation but no other matching company details; there is a risk the organisation may be attempting to clone another legitimate organisation.

   LOW RISK  
Shell company indentification
The organisation displays a significant number of characteristics associated with a Shell company and may be concealing important information and its true purpose and identity.
Help with shell company indentification

Risk: This RISK ID indicates whether an organisation may be a shell company. The entities could be concealing important information and its true purpose and identity.

  LOW RISK   The organisation has minimal characteristics that can be associated with a shell company.

  MEDIUM RISK   The organisation has some of the characteristics that can be associated with a shell company.

  HIGH RISK   The organisation has some of the characteristics that can be associated with a shell company.

   HIGH RISK
